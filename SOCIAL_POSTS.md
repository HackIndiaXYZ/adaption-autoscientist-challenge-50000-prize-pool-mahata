## LinkedIn (tag Adaption / adaption-labs) — no length limit, post in full

> **We taught a 70B model to speak Banglish — and it beats the baseline 65% of the time.**
>
> "kal ki plan? ami free achi" — this is how 100M+ Bengali speakers actually
> type. Romanized, code-mixed, no standard spelling (ache/ase/achhe all mean
> the same thing). Off-the-shelf LLMs fall apart on it.
>
> For the Adaption AutoScientist Challenge × HackIndia, Team MAHATA built
> **BanglaBridge-Instruct**:
>
> 🔹 An original, 100% hand-authored instruction dataset across all three real
> registers — romanized, native script, and code-switch — with deterministic
> spelling-variation augmentation so the model learns meaning, not surface form
> 🔹 Quality-lifted through Adaption's Adaptive Data pipeline
> 🔹 Fine-tuned from Llama 3.3 70B Instruct with AutoScientist's self-learning
> training loop
> 🔹 **65% win rate against the baseline** on Adaption's held-out test set
>
> Everything is released openly — weights and dataset on both Hugging Face and
> Kaggle:
> 🤗 https://huggingface.co/subhajitmahata84/BanglaBridge-Instruct
> 📄 https://huggingface.co/datasets/subhajitmahata84/banglabridge-instructions
